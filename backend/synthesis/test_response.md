## Answer

The Species Habitat Index (SHI) measures the average proportion of suitable habitat remaining for a set of species relative to a baseline year. Values range from 0 to 1, where 1 indicates no habitat loss since the baseline year (Source: BON in a Box: Species Habitat Index Documentation, chunk bon-shi-001). For The Hague Groenmonitor, the SHI score decreased from 0.82 in 2019 to 0.77 in 2024, indicating a decline in the reported index over this period (Source: SHI Score Table 2019-2024 — The Hague Groenmonitor, chunk bon-shi-002).
